Smokingrill Cafe

Smokingrill is located at the edge of Mirpur Azad Kashmir. The cafe name is inspiration, taken from many years of grill work of our chef. We have variety of food served from grilled till home made. Our chefs are highly trained and motivated whereas, goal is only to create best food of town. At Smokingrill cafe, the Asian ingredients are the focus, but with us everything must be possible - that's why we like to be inspired by the rest of the world. We use the season's fresh ingredients and prepare the food from scratch - and always with attention to detail. We use all parts of the raw material and thus avoid food waste, and we think about ecology and sustainability.
